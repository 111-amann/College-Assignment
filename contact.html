Find Us on Campus

Computer Science Building

Directions

By Car

Take Main Street to University Avenue. Turn left and proceed for 0.5 miles. The Computer Science building is on your right. Visitor parking available in Lot C.

Public Transit

Take Bus Route 15 or 22 to the University College stop. The CS building is a 3-minute walk from the bus stop.

On Campus

From the Main Library, head north towards the Science Quad. The Computer Science building is located next to the Engineering building.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to the Computer Science program?

Applications can be submitted through our online portal. Visit our courses page for detailed admission requirements and deadlines.

What are the prerequisites for graduate programs?

A bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field with a minimum GPA of 3.0. Additional requirements vary by program level.

Are research opportunities available for undergraduates?

Yes! We encourage undergraduate research participation. Contact faculty members directly or reach out to our research coordinator.

How can I schedule a campus visit?

Contact our admissions office at (555) 123-4570 to schedule a personalized campus tour and meet with faculty and current students.
