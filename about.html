About Our Department

Excellence in Computer Science Education Since 1985

Our Mission

The Computer Science Department at Sanjivani University is dedicated to providing world-class education in computing and technology. We strive to develop skilled professionals who can contribute meaningfully to the rapidly evolving field of computer science.

Our mission encompasses three core pillars: innovative teaching methodologies, cutting-edge research, and strong industry partnerships that prepare our students for successful careers in technology.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading computer science department that shapes the future of technology through exceptional education, groundbreaking research, and meaningful community engagement.

We envision our graduates as innovative leaders who drive technological advancement while maintaining ethical responsibility and social consciousness in their professional endeavors.

Department History

Established in 1985, our Computer Science Department began with just 5 faculty members and 50 students. Over the decades, we have grown exponentially while maintaining our commitment to academic excellence.

Key milestones in our journey include the establishment of our first computer lab in 1987, the introduction of graduate programs in 1995, and the launch of our research centers in 2005.
